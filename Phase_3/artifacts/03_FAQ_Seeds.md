# Top-20 FAQ Seed List

These questions will be used to populate the database for the initial pilot and AI testing.

| ID | Category | Question | Draft Answer Key Points |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | Payments | How do I pay with MPESA? | Go to checkout -> Select MPESA -> Enter number -> Confirm on phone. |
| 2 | Payments | What if my payment fails? | Check balance, check network, wait 5 mins, contact support. |
| 3 | Payments | How do I request a refund? | Go to Orders -> Select Order -> Click "Request Refund". Only within 48 hours. |
| 4 | Payments | Do you accept credit cards? | Yes, Visa and Mastercard are supported. |
| 5 | Listings | How do I create a listing? | Click "Sell" button -> Upload photos -> Add description -> Set price. |
| 6 | Listings | Can I edit my listing after posting? | Yes, go to "My Listings" -> Click "Edit". Price changes require approval. |
| 7 | Listings | How many photos can I upload? | Minimum 3, Maximum 10. High quality recommended. |
| 8 | Listings | Why was my listing rejected? | Check prohibited items list. Usually due to low quality photos or banned items. |
| 9 | Safety | How do I report a scam? | Click "Report User" on their profile or listing. We investigate within 24 hours. |
| 10 | Safety | What are buyer protections? | We hold funds until you confirm receipt of item (Escrow). |
| 11 | Safety | Is it safe to meet sellers in person? | Only meet in public places. Do not carry large cash amounts. |
| 12 | Disputes | The item is not as described. | Open a dispute immediately. Do not release funds. Upload photos of item. |
| 13 | Disputes | Seller is not responding. | If >24 hours after payment, contact support to cancel order. |
| 14 | Account | How do I reset my password? | Click "Forgot Password" on login screen -> Check email for link. |
| 15 | Account | Can I change my email address? | Yes, in Settings -> Profile. Requires verification of new email. |
| 16 | Account | How do I delete my account? | Contact support. We retain data for 30 days for legal reasons. |
| 17 | Delivery | Who pays for shipping? | Buyer pays unless Seller offers "Free Shipping". |
| 18 | Delivery | How do I track my order? | Go to Orders -> Click "Track". Status updates from courier. |
| 19 | Delivery | Delivery is delayed. | Check tracking. If stuck >3 days, contact support. |
| 20 | General | How do I contact support? | Use the "Ask a Question" form or email support@somba.com. |
